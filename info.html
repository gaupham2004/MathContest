About the Mathema Contest

What is the Mathema Contest?

The Mathema Contest is a South African based international online math competition that seeks to enhance the importance of math education worldwide while equipping young people with critical mathematical skills. Open to individuals and teams across a variety of age groups, it fosters problem-solving, advanced thinking, and love for mathematics globally.

How the Contest Works

The Mathema Contest runs twice per year:

  • Competition 1: March – June
  • Competition 2: August – November

Each competition has three rounds:

  • Round 1: Qualifying
  • Round 2: Semi-finals
  • Round 3: Finals
